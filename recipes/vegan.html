Vegan Recipe:

Rice and Beans

This week's vegan recipe is an all time classic in every culture: rice and beans

Ingredients

  • Basmasti rice
  • 14oz can of pinto beans
  • One medium sweet onion
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper
  • Avocado oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Wash 1 cup of rice and cook in 1.5 cups of water on low heat
  2. Cut the onion into small chunks
  3. Cook onion in oil for 10 minutes
  4. Add drained pinto beans and cook for another 10 minutes
  5. Add 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  6. Salt and pepper to taste
  7. Serve beans with rice
