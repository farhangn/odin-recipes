5 Minute Recipe:

Nut Butter Sandwich

This week's meat-based recipe is an all time classic in every culture: nut butter sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp of a nut butter of your choice
  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Optional: 2 tbsp of a fruit jam

Cooking Instructions

  1. Toash the bread slices on both sides
  2. Spread 1 tbsp of nut butter on each side
  3. Optional: Spread 1 tbsp of fruit jam on each side
  4. Close the sandwich and serve
