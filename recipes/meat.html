Meat-Based Recipe:

Rice and Beef

This week's meat-based recipe is an all time classic in every culture: beef and beans

Ingredients

  • Basmasti rice
  • 1 lbs of ground beef
  • One medium sweet onion
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper
  • Avocado oil
  • Optional: 1/2 cup of chopped green onions

Cooking Instructions

  1. Wash 1 cup of rice and cook in 1.5 cups of water on low heat
  2. Cut the onion into small chunks
  3. Cook onion in oil for 10 minutes
  4. Add the ground beef in chunks and cook for 30 minutes
  5. Add 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  6. Salt and pepper to taste
  7. Serve beans with rice
  8. Optional: add green onions for garnish
